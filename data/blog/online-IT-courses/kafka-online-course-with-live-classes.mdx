### What is Apache Kafka?

Apache Kafka is an open-source distributed event streaming platform that can handle trillions of events per day from thousands of sources. It is used by more than 80% of all Fortune 100 companies, including Uber, Netflix, LinkedIn, Twitter, and many more1

### Kafka enables you to:

Collect and store streams of data safely and reliably in a distributed, durable, fault-tolerant cluster.
Process and transform streams of data with joins, aggregations, filters, transformations, and more, using event-time and exactly-once processing.
Connect to hundreds of external systems for data import and export, such as databases, message queues, cloud services, etc.
Build scalable and resilient applications that can react to events in real-time and deliver business value.

Apache Kafka is a distributed streaming platform that enables producers and consumers to
