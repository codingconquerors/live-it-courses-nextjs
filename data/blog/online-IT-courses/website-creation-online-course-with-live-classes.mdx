## Course Outline

This course covers A to Z of Android. At the end of this course you will learn each and every aspect of Android app development with hands on experience on several Android apps.
Duration of the course is of 8 weeks. Each of the below topics is covered in live classes. Every problem students face is resolved on the spot.

1. Introduction to Android Framework and configuring Android development environment.
2. Various components of Android.
3. Understanding components life cycle.
4. Understanding user interactions in Android: Messages, Lists, Views and Layouts.
5. Shared preferences in Android.
6. Internationalization in Android.
7. Supporting different screen sizes in Android.
8. Publishing your app on google play.
9. SQLite database crud operation.
10. Sending data between activities.
11. Exception handling in Android.
12. Handling notifications in Android.
13. Understanding firebase database.
14. Invoking Rest API in and Android application.
15. Understanding Intents and Filters.
16. Implementing services and different types of services.
17. Android multimedia: Play audio, video in your app.
18. Sending and receiving SMS and MMS.
19. Using the bluetooth api.
